General Tips

  • If the location is far, go to the nearest stagecoachable dock, then take the stagecoach.
  • Leave a mark if you think you will return or if the location is far from the dock.
  • If it's not connected, use "port to life fountain" then go to stagecoach or skyway.
  • Subata Temple Docks is your general hub for Subata Skyway.
  • Khotan Dock is your general hub for Khotan Skyway.
  • Hamamitsu Garden Docks is your general hub for Hamamitsu Skyway.
  • Try reading this guide as you reach each new location so you can grab all the good gear, companions, and more as you go and save yourself the trips back to Mooshu.
  • Please grab the Zeke quest as soon as you get in the world! You can find him in Hamamitsu Garden. Collect the knives as you visit each new area. This will save you time, as there are a huge number of Mooshu knives. In return for the quest, Zeke will give you a practice point for training powers and epics.
